Week 2

The second task was to build three live charts and embed them onto my page. The first two are ran directly from my GitHub, and the third chart delivered directly through an API.

IMF Credit Outstanding

This chart hosts CSV data on IMF credit over time.

IMF Credit Relative to GDP

This chart divides the IMF credit data above by respective GDP to show IMF lending as a percentage of GDP.

This chart reduces the danger of data misrepresentation the previous graph has. 1984 is the year the IMF lent most, relative to GDP, whereas the previous chart depicts 2022 as the most credit lent, when lending is at a peak only in absolute terms.
